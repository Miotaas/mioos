# MioOS Business OS — Overview

MioOS Business OS is the internal operating layer for selling and deploying five AI agent products to clients. It manages every step from the first lead to live deployment and ongoing support.

## The Five AI Agent Products

| Product | Core Value | Status |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Follow-Up & Action Watchdog | Never miss a follow-up or open action | Demo-ready |
| Deadline & Document Guardian | Monitor all contracts and compliance deadlines | Demo-ready |
| Lost Revenue Detector | Find stalled quotes, unpaid invoices, cold proposals | Demo-ready |
| Meeting-to-Execution Agent | Turn meeting notes into tasks and decisions automatically | Pilot-ready |
| Evidence & Case Builder | Organize evidence and case documentation | Building |

## Lead-to-Live Process

```
Lead
 └─► Product Match (deterministic rules on pain point)
      └─► Outreach
           └─► Discovery Call
                └─► Demo
                     └─► Proposal
                          └─► Pilot (2–3 weeks)
                               └─► Onboarding
                                    └─► Deployment
                                         └─► Support
                                              └─► Upsell
```

## How Sales Partner Uses It

1. Open MioOS → Leads view → filter by "needs follow-up today"
2. Check Pipeline view for overdue leads at each stage
3. Use "Suggest Product" on a lead's pain point to get demo prep instantly
4. Track proposal and pilot status directly on the lead
5. When pilot converts → lead becomes "won" → onboarding checklist activates
6. Create Deployment record for each live client
7. Log support issues as they arise, link to client and product
8. Track upsell opportunities when patterns emerge

## Key Pages

- **Products** — The 5 AI agent products with full detail, demo angles, pricing
- **Leads** — Full CRM with pain point matching, demo prep, proposal/pilot tracking
- **Pipeline** — Grouped view of all leads by sales stage
- **Deployments** — Track each live or in-progress deployment
- **Support** — Issue tracker linked to clients and products
