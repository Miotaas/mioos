# MioOS Product Catalog

## 1. Follow-Up & Action Watchdog

**What it does:** Monitors open follow-ups, pending replies, and action items across email and CRM. Surfaces what needs attention before deadlines slip.

**Target customer:** Sales teams, account managers, consultancies, solopreneurs with high email volume.

**Core pain point:** "We lose deals because we forget to follow up. Things fall through the cracks."

**Demo angle:** Show 3 real open follow-ups surfaced automatically. Let them feel the relief of nothing being forgotten.

**Pricing range:** €300–600/month

**MVP scope:** Email + CRM integration, daily digest, overdue alert, action item tracking.

---

## 2. Deadline & Document Guardian

**What it does:** Reads contracts and documents, extracts deadlines, and sends alerts before anything expires or auto-renews unexpectedly.

**Target customer:** Legal teams, finance departments, compliance officers, operations managers.

**Core pain point:** "We missed a contract renewal that cost us thousands. We track deadlines in spreadsheets."

**Demo angle:** Show a contract renewal that would have been missed — flagged 30 days early, with one-click action.

**Pricing range:** €400–800/month

**MVP scope:** Document upload, deadline extraction, alert schedule (30/14/7 days), dashboard.

---

## 3. Lost Revenue Detector

**What it does:** Connects to invoicing and CRM to identify stalled quotes, unpaid invoices, and cold proposals — then surfaces them daily.

**Target customer:** SMB owners, sales directors, freelancers, B2B companies with 30+ day sales cycles.

**Core pain point:** "I have invoices 60+ days overdue. Quotes go cold and we never follow up. Revenue is leaking."

**Demo angle:** Pull up their last 30 days of quotes. Show which ones have gone cold and estimate the total revenue at risk.

**Pricing range:** €500–1,000/month

**MVP scope:** Invoicing integration (Exact, Moneybird, Billit), overdue flagging, recovery digest, pipeline leak report.

---

## 4. Meeting-to-Execution Agent

**What it does:** Converts meeting notes or transcripts into a structured list of decisions, action items, owners, and deadlines.

**Target customer:** Operations teams, project managers, management consultants, executive assistants.

**Core pain point:** "After every meeting we spend 30 minutes on notes. Things still get missed. Actions don't happen."

**Demo angle:** Take a real meeting notes file. Show how the agent produces a structured action list in under 60 seconds.

**Pricing range:** €350–700/month

**MVP scope:** Paste notes or upload transcript, extract actions with owners + deadlines, export to task system.

---

## 5. Evidence & Case Builder

**What it does:** Organizes evidence, claims, dispute documentation, and communications into structured, timestamped case files.

**Target customer:** Legal professionals, HR departments, insurers, compliance teams, property managers.

**Core pain point:** "When a dispute escalates, we can't find the right evidence. Emails are everywhere. Documentation is a mess."

**Demo angle:** Build a demo case from realistic emails and documents. Show how a messy dispute becomes a clean, searchable evidence file.

**Pricing range:** €600–1,200/month

**MVP scope:** Case file creation, evidence upload and tagging, timeline view, chronological export to PDF.
